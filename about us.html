ORGANISATION BACKGROUND

We are Chung Ling High School Choir Club and we are family

Welcome to the Chung Ling High School Choir Club website! We are a group of passionate singers who come together to share our love for music and performance.

Our choir club was founded in 1997, and since then, we have been performing in various events and competitions. We are proud to have won numerous awards and accolades over the years, including the gold medal at the Malaysia Choral Eisteddfod and the champion title at the Penang State Choral Competition.

At Chung Ling High School Choir Club, we believe in nurturing our members' musical talents and providing them with opportunities to grow and excel. Our members come from diverse backgrounds and have different levels of experience, but we all share a common goal: to sing quality song that touches the hearts of our audience.

If you are interested in joining our choir club, we welcome singers of all ages and skill levels. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced vocalist, we have a place for you in our choir family.

WHY JOIN US?

As a member of our choir club, you will have the opportunity to:

• Learn from experienced vocal coaches.

• Get more curriculum marks as we got join in various competitions.

• Perform in various events and competitions.

• Make new friends who share your passion for music.

• Develop your musical skills and build your confidence as a performer.
